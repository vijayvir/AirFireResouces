Glowing Border


Changing Expectations provides the highest impact STEM, computer science, and artificial intelligence education to inspire primarily Black and Hispanic youth in K-16 to pursue computing careers and tech entrepreneurship and to support their teachers in underserved schools and at HBCUs. Our programs are designed to study and increase Black and Hispanic students and their teachers' interest, engagement, learning, knowledge, sense of belonging and identity, and intentions to persist in STEM and computer science education, including artificial intelligence and cybersecurity.
